**Bangkok, Thailand** — ETIA Technology is proud to announce the launch of **Etiatec (Thailand) Co., Ltd.**, our dedicated Thailand operation, appointed by **Excelitas Canada Inc.** as an authorized distributor of **OmniCure® UV curing systems** in Thailand.

Thai manufacturers in medical devices, electronics, automotive and photonics no longer need to source UV curing equipment overseas — genuine OmniCure systems, spare lamps and accessories are now stocked, installed and serviced locally, with support in Thai.

## What this means for manufacturers in Thailand

- **Genuine products, local stock** — authorized supply of OmniCure spot curing systems, lamps, light guides and radiometers, without long overseas lead times.
- **Installation and process support** — on-site setup, process validation and operator training for production lines.
- **Calibration and measurement** — radiometry and calibration support with the R2000 and LS200, so a validated cure process stays validated.
- **Maintenance and repair** — troubleshooting, spare parts and repair coordination to keep downtime short.
- **Thai-language technical support** — engineers can discuss adhesives, substrates and cure windows in their own language.

## Systems available now

- **[OmniCure S2000 Elite](/omnicure-s2000)** — the flagship 200 W lamp-based spot curing system with Closed-Loop Feedback, up to 30 W/cm².
- **[OmniCure S1500 Pro](/omnicure-s1500-pro)** — automation-ready spot curing with a guaranteed 2,000-hour lamp, PLC output and Industry 4.0 traceability.
- **[OmniCure LX500](/omnicure-lx500)** — ultra-compact UV LED spot curing with ±5% optical stability and up to 27 W/cm².
- Plus genuine lamps, light guides, optical filters and radiometers.

## About ETIA Technology

ETIA Technology is an authorized distributor of UV curing systems with **20 years of application expertise**, serving manufacturers from offices in Shanghai, Hong Kong, Bangkok and Bac Ninh. We support the full equipment lifecycle — selection, process validation, installation, calibration, maintenance and repair.
